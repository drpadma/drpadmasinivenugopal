About

Hello! I'm Padmasini, and I'm a professional with a unique blend of expertise in dentistry and data analysis. My journey from a dedicated dentist to a passionate data analyst has been nothing short of remarkable. Allow me to share my story with you.


My career began in the healthcare sector, where I developed my skills as a dentist for the past 4 years. During my time in dentistry, I developed a keen eye for detail, precision, and the importance of data in making informed decisions. I learned how data-driven decisions could improve patient care and outcomes. This fascination with data eventually led me down a new path.


Later, after recognizing the power of data, I decided to pivot my career towards data analysis. I embarked on a journey of acquiring skills in statistics, programming languages, and data visualization. This transition was challenging, but my background in healthcare instilled in me a strong work ethic, resilience, and a commitment to excellence that served me well in my new endeavor.


Today, I am a dedicated data analyst with a passion for uncovering insights, solving complex problems, and making data-driven recommendations. My background in dentistry has given me a unique perspective, allowing me to approach data analysis with precision and a focus on the finer details. I thrive on translating data into meaningful insights that drive business decisions.


Beyond the work, I love dancing, playing, and traveling. These activities help me maintain a healthy work-life balance. I'm excited about the opportunities to combine my healthcare background with my analytical skills to make a positive impact.

Projects

GameCo's Sales Analysis.

As I developed my foundation, I worked with GameCo's sales statistics on video games. I was asked to perform an end-to-end analysis of data from 1980 to 2017 in order to get insights for boosting global sales. I began my analysis using the industry standard tool "Excel" based on the project brief provided by the company. This analysis involved data cleaning (removing duplicates, missing data, and incorrect data), analytical methods to derive insights, and visualization using pivot tables and the various charts offered by Excel. Lastly, presented it to the stakeholders as a presentation.

Influenza Mortality Analysis.

Data sets includes CDC influenza death data and US census data. The requirement instructed to conduct an analysis of both sets of data by integrating them together in order to determine the most effective way to disperse agency personnel across the US in order to reduce influenza mortality in the future year. Excel was used to start my analysis in accordance with the project brief. I performed all the fundamental analyses using Excel. Finally, I used Tableau to create a data presentation.

Rockbuster Video Rental Sales Analysis.

Here, I used Postgres and RDBMS to access data from the Rockbuster database, which houses video rentals. By following the creation of an ERD (Entity relationship diagram), I then performed various queries in Postgres using the SQL commands to filter the data, performed a thorough analysis, joined different tables, and performed subqueries/Common table expressions to pull out various information from different tables and downloaded the data from Postgres as a CSV file for further analysis In the end, I prepared a presentation with all of my analytical insights for enhancing sales after using Tableau to create visualizations.

Instacart, Online grocery

Instacart is an online grocery store that operates through an app in the US. The analysis is done to understand the sales pattern, finding the ordering habits of customers based on demographic details and regions to create targeted marketing campaigns.

Dairy data Analysis in India (2019-2022)

The dataset provides a detailed and comprehensive collection of data related to dairy farms, dairy products, sales, and inventory management. It contains information from various brands and milk products of India over the past 4 years and wants to understand the sales pattern to improvise the pricing strategies among different brands.
